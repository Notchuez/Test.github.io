Monthly Classes at Gold Standard Gym

Monthly classes at the Gold Standard are our pride and joy! By and far the most popular feature in our gym, these classes cover a wide range of displines and offer something for everyone.

To Start our month off we feature our bi-weekly Yoga Class. Come and get the stretch and loosening you need for that busy month ahead! we also present a series of Conditioning Classes for those who are more actively oriented.

Keeping in mind, and in use of our beautiful weightlifitng equipment, we also feature our bi-monthly Big Lifts Classes with Olympian Weightlifter Bill Brutus. These classes are geared for more advanced lifters in mind.

We also present a variety of new classes! We are proud to present our brand new Pilates Classes, our brand new instructor is an industry veteran and will def make you work for that burn.

Finally, To wrap up our busy schedule we have our monthly Monthly Members Part, come and meet our instructors, staff and your fellow fitness friends!
